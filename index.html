About

GEMILINK

Gemilink is a spin-off from SRETT, which was established in 2007 to develop long-range, low power IoT solutions for the industry and homecare monitoring solutions for the medical industry. Aiming to become more customer-centric, SRETT strategically decided to split its business activities in 2021 and created two companies - Vestalis for the medical Industry, and GEMILINK.

Today, Gemilink innovates and focuses on engineering and IoT solutions that support Energy Transition via IoT Advanced Technologies. Our pressure and temperature sensors developed under the brand name ‘BumbleBee’ continue to serve with pride as our company’s hero product.
